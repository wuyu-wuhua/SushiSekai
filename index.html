Game Strategy Guide

Beginner's Guide

Start with simple rolls like California rolls and cucumber rolls to build your confidence. Focus on perfecting the rice preparation - it's the foundation of all sushi!

  • Keep your rice at the perfect temperature
  • Use the right amount of rice vinegar
  • Practice your rolling technique

Advanced Techniques

Once you've mastered the basics, try these advanced techniques to create more complex and beautiful sushi creations!

  • Master the art of nigiri preparation
  • Learn to create decorative garnishes
  • Experiment with fusion flavors

Restaurant Management Tips

Running a successful sushi restaurant requires more than just cooking skills. Here are some tips to help you manage your virtual restaurant effectively!

  • Balance your menu with popular and specialty items
  • Manage your inventory efficiently
  • Train your staff to maintain quality
  • Create an inviting atmosphere for customers

Game Development History

January 2022

The Idea Was Born

Our team came together with a vision to create a game that would celebrate Japanese cuisine while providing an engaging gaming experience!

March 2022

Development Begins

We started developing the core mechanics and creating the initial art assets for the game with a focus on cute and appealing visuals!

August 2022

Alpha Testing

The first playable version was released to a select group of testers who provided valuable feedback to help us improve the game!

December 2022

Beta Release

We expanded testing to a wider audience and implemented improvements based on player feedback to make the game even more enjoyable!

March 2023

Official Launch

The game was officially released to the public, receiving positive reviews and a growing player base of sushi enthusiasts!

How to Play Sushi Sekai

Getting Started

Begin your journey as a sushi chef by selecting your favorite recipes and ingredients. Follow the step-by-step instructions to create perfect sushi rolls with cute animations!

Game Controls

Use your mouse to select ingredients and drag them to the preparation area. Click to perform cooking actions and create your masterpiece with satisfying visual feedback!

Scoring System

Earn points based on your cooking speed, accuracy, and presentation. Unlock new recipes and ingredients as you progress, with adorable rewards and achievements!
